Composition rules (do these by default)

Every slide is rendered at 1920 × 1080 and scaled to fit the viewport.
Top-bar pattern: section pill (left) + Yuno wordmark (right). Don't skip the pill — it anchors the deck rhythm.
Headlines use the gradient-title treatment ONLY on the noun being emphasized (1 phrase per slide max).
Cards prefer --card-gradient; the "focus" card on a row gets .border-beam + --card-gradient-accent.
Glow uses --glow-blue (sparingly — at most one source of glow per slide).
Accent green #E0ED80 is reserved for "live / ok / success" — never for emphasis.
Numerals always use font-variant-numeric: tabular-nums for stat blocks.
Padding scale (stage): clamp(28px,3.6%,64px) vertical / clamp(36px,4.8%,90px) horizontal.
Respect @media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce) — ambient drifts and ring spins disable themselves.
